# ADR 0001: Site purpose and information architecture

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Rebuild of Paintings By Chantal portfolio. Need a clear primary job and route structure before UI/tech choices.

## Decision

- Primary job: **brand home** (identity, artwork, contact in equal weight).
- Structure: **multi-page** with Home as a **brand landing** (no full galleries).
- Gallery pages: **masonry + lightbox**.
- Contact: **links + email CTA** (no form).
- Visual: **studio quiet**; motion: **whisper**.

## Consequences

Home funnels into About / Oils / Sketches / Contact. Galleries own browsing; Contact stays lightweight.
